Admissions at VIT Vellore

VIT offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs across various disciplines. Our admissions process ensures that we select the best minds.

Programs Offered:

  • B.Tech in various specializations
  • M.Tech in advanced technology fields
  • PhD programs with research opportunities

Admission Process:

Admissions are based on merit through VITEEE for B.Tech and VITMEE for M.Tech. Direct admissions are available for certain courses.

Scholarships:

We offer scholarships to meritorious students and those with financial constraints. Apply now to check eligibility.
