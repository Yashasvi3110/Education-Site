Welcome to VIT Vellore

Empowering students with knowledge, innovation, and excellence in education.

VIT Vellore is a globally recognized university known for its commitment to academic excellence, cutting-edge research, and holistic student development. With a legacy of over three decades, we have been shaping future leaders and innovators.

Admissions

Join a community of scholars and innovators. Learn about our admission requirements and start your journey with VIT Vellore.

Our admission process is designed to identify talented students who have the potential to excel in their chosen fields. Each year, thousands of students from across the country and abroad apply to VIT for its rigorous academic programs and dynamic learning environment.

Academics

Explore our diverse programs and find the right fit for your future. Our curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking and creativity.

VIT offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines, including engineering, business, humanities, and sciences. Our academic structure encourages interdisciplinary learning and research.

Our faculty comprises highly qualified professors, researchers, and industry experts who bring real-world knowledge into the classroom. The university's state-of-the-art labs and research centers provide students with hands-on experience in their respective fields.

Student Life

Experience a vibrant campus life with numerous clubs, organizations, and events that make your university years unforgettable.

At VIT, learning goes beyond the classroom. Students can participate in over 100 clubs and organizations, ranging from technical societies and cultural clubs to sports teams and social service groups. These activities foster leadership, teamwork, and personal growth.

The campus is equipped with modern amenities, including hostels, a world-class library, sports facilities, and dining options. Regular cultural and technical festivals bring students together to celebrate innovation, creativity, and diversity.
